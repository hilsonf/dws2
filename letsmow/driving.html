Drivers Tips

  1. Look at least two seconds in front - The vehicle you are trailing will be in your peripheral vision. Focus on the road ahead for any gaps/ openings to pass your opponent
  2. Brake Late - Braking late can be dangerous but the likelihood of you catching up to the leader is improved 10X
  3. Make your hands as smooth as silk - Don't force it!! Your grip should be firm but sill gentle enough to corner around the tight bends
